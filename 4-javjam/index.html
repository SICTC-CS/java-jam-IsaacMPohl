Follow The Winding Road to JavaJam

We're a little out of the way, but take a drive in the country down Garrett Bay Road to JavaJam today! Indulge in our locally roasted free-trade coffee and home-made pastries. You'll fell right at home at JavaJam!

Friendly and eclectic - JavaJam Coffe Bar is the perfect place to take a break, enjoy a refreshing beverage, and have a snack or light meal.

JavaJam Coffee Bar features:

  • Specialty Coffee and Organic Tea
  • Bagals, Muffins, and Gluten-free Pastries
  • Organic Salads
  • Music and Poetry Reading
  • Open Mic Night
